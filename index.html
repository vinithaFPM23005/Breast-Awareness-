Breast Cancer Awareness & Self-Examination

Early detection is the first step toward a cure. पहचान जो जीवन बचाऐ

Key Facts

  • Breast cancer occurs when abnormal cells grow in the breast[cite: 5].
  • Monthly self-examination helps early identification[cite: 7].
  • Confidential and supportive care is available[cite: 10].

What to Look For

During your monthly check, look for[cite: 8]:

  • Lumps or thickening in the breast[cite: 8].
  • Changes in size or shape[cite: 8].
  • Unusual pain or skin changes[cite: 8].
